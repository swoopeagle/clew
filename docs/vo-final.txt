CLEW DEMO NARRATION (read at a calm pace, about 2:50)

Millions of teams run their day inside Slack, and that includes nonprofits.
There are over one and a half million nonprofits in the United States alone,
and most of them are tiny. A couple of people wearing every hat, doing the
work of entire departments. They live and die by grant funding, but finding
grants is slow, confusing, and the tools that help cost hundreds of dollars a
month. So the search for money becomes the first thing that falls off the
desk, at exactly the organizations that can least afford it.

We built Clew to fix that. Clew is a Slack agent that works like a full grant
office. It searches federal opportunities, foundation tax records, and real
award data, and surfaces extremely high quality grants that are hyper
tailored to your organization. And it has one rule: it can never recommend a
funder it can't prove exists. Every prospect comes with a real citation, and
that rule is enforced in code, not in a prompt.

Here's what that looks like for Paws for Purple Hearts, a nonprofit that
trains service dogs for wounded veterans. Setup is one message. Paste your
website, and Clew drafts your entire profile. Ask it to find grants, and it
sweeps every source and returns a cited shortlist matched to your mission,
your geography, and your capacity. Click a citation, and you're looking at a
live government record.

Approve a grant, and Clew opens a war room. The deadline sits in the channel
topic, and the brief already holds the application portal, the criteria, a
contact, and an honest answer on whether you're even eligible. Click Draft
Application, and it writes a competitive draft into a shared canvas, marking
exactly what's ready to use and exactly what it needs from you. It proposes
the action items, you hand each one to a teammate with a click, and every
morning it briefs the team and nudges whatever's falling behind.

And here's the bigger vision. Thousands of nonprofits already run on
Salesforce Nonprofit Cloud, but the real collaboration happens in chat. Clew
is the actionable bridge, the place where funding data becomes actual work,
and continuity between Slack and Salesforce is our next step.

Nonprofits do the most with the least. With Clew, Slack becomes the teammate
they could never afford to hire. Let's make the world a better place with
Slack agents.
